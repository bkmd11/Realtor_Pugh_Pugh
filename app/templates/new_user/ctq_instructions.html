It is time to set your "Critical To Quality" attributes!

What that means is setting the criteria that is really most important to you in your home. These are going to be different for every individual or couple looking to purchase, so it is very important that you take the time to reflect on what is really important. Things like "Two car garage" or "Ranch style" do not make good CTQ's given that you probably wont even look at houses that don't have those. But things like "Proximity to such and such activity" or "Privacy" could make good CTQ's. Those attributes tend to be a little less obvious in a listing. Taking some time to discuss and establish good CTQ's will give you much more confidence when it finally comes time to make the big purchase
